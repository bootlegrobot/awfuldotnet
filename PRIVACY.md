Privacy Policy
==============

Personal Information Collection and Use
---------------------------------------

When you login, Awful recieves a cookie from the SomethingAwful Forums and stores this on your device. Your password is never visible to Awful and never stored on your device. Awful does not save or use a user’s personal information. User account data is not shared with third parties or used for any other purpose. Any file you upload using Awful is stored on Imgur anonymously. Awful does not monitor or view your personal information or Content stored on Imgur.
